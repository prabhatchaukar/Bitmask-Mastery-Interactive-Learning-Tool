üí° Key Concepts to Remember

üîπ OR (|): At least one bit is 1 ‚Üí Result is 1
üîπ XOR (^): Bits are different ‚Üí Result is 1
üîπ NOT (~): Flips all bits (0‚Üí1, 1‚Üí0)
